Hesitant to change
- Management companies have been doing it the same way for decades

Specific forms need to be digitized
- There are unique forms for each organization that need to be signed directly. How to upload these?
  - Common forms, like carbon monoxide and window guard, can be uploaded once and archived, so other agencies can use them.

Lowers margins on application
- Taking a cut of the application fee could be scary to some management companies.
  - They need to see benefit of saving time and increasing the # of applications/apt
